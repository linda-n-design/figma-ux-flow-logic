About

UX Flow Logic is a Figma plugin for creating annotated user flow diagrams. It gives you a sticker sheet of standard flowchart symbols, lets you build your flow visually, and then automatically renumbers everything with one click. It also helps you document triggers, assumptions, and conditions—all the context developers need for handoff.
